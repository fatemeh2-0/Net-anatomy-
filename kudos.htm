Kudos

(Praise given for achievement - Webster's Collegiate Dictionary, 10th ed.)

NetAnatomy was selected by Scientific American as one of the top 50 science and technology web sites for 2004, garnishing NetAnatomy a coveted 2004 Science & Technology Web Award. NetAnatomy was only one of five web sites selected in the Health & Medicine category.
NetAnatomy was independently evaluated by an editorial board of ISI (Institute for Scientific Information). Based on a strict set of evaluation criteria that emphasizes quality, design, and accuracy, NetAnatomy was chosen for inclusion in ISI's Current Web Contents.

NetAnatomy is a recipient of the med411.com "Medical Award" based on selection criteria that emphasizes content of interest to healthcare professionals presented with a high degree of integrity and credibility and a tone, site structure, navigation, and graphic design at a professional level.
